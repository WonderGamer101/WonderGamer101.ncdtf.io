Welcome to the Non-Canon Dream Tourney Festival!

Rules:

  • Three stocks, 8 minutes, no items, Team Attack off (except for grudge matches), best of one (most of the time)

  • The winning team rep earns 1 point for every victory, the team with most points wins.

  • Whenever a three-stock, reverse three-stock, or a JV3 occurs, the team suffering from it will have the opportunity to challenge the other in a "double-or-nothing" fight. The winner of that match will earn 2 points instead of 1.
